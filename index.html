Event Schedule

28 Feb 2021

Opening Ceremony
10-11 am PST

We're going to start the hackathon with a bang! All important information about the hackathon will be discussed in this presentation. All participants are required to attend.

Team Mixer
2-3 pm PST

Hackathons are a great way to meet new people! You can meet other coders with similar interests.

01 Mar 2021

DevOps Workshop
8:00-8:30 pm PST

"Machine learning" and "Artificial intelligence" seem to be the brightest buzzwords today in the tech world. However, a concept that's been gaining as much traction within Silicon Valley is DevOps. In this workshop, you'll learn what DevOps is, how companies use it, and how you can get started with DevOps tools!

02 Mar 2021

EchoAR Workshop
5-6 pm PST

This workshop will show how to quickly create augmented and virtual reality (AR/VR) apps with no technical skills or coding required and scale existing apps by connecting them to the cloud. Workshop participants will learn how to create real-time cloud-connected AR/VR apps using the echoAR platform. Participants are encouraged to follow along with the live demonstration. Platforms for experimentation include: Google ARCore, WebXR, Vuforia, Unity-based apps, and more.

03 Mar 2021

How to land an Internship
5-6 pm PST

In this presentation, Jeremy Rossman will teach participants how to land their first internship. Get ahead of your competition!

05 Mar 2021

Machine Learning Workshop
6 pm PST

An introduction to machine learning with a quickdraw emoji pediction feature. We will be discussing core concepts of machine learning!

06 Mar 2021

Submission and Judging
9 am - 12 pm PST

Hackers will submit their projects to the judges and give a presentation on their final product. Be ready!

Closing Ceremony
3 pm PST

Awards will be given out, and the event will be wrapped up.
